Huaiyang Cuisine

Huaiyang cuisine originates from Yangzhou and Huai'an. Most Huaiyang cuisines are made of freshwater fish, shrimp and crabs. Huaiyang Chef has the finest knifework. The dishes are exquisite in shape, focusing on the luxury of dishes and the complexity of skills.

Huaiyang cuisine has a mellow taste, likes to use sauce and sugar, and uses more oil for cooking.
