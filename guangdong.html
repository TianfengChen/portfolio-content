Guangdong Cuisine

A large number of Guangdong immigrants and the status of a window of communication between China and the West have made Cantonese cuisine continuously absorb Western cooking skills. Cantonese chefs adhere to the flexible and divergent way of thinking of Cantonese. They have a quality, that is, they are always endlessly studying all edible things and how to make them delicious and eat better.

Guangdong Morning Tea

It is a unique tradition of Cantonese to be keen on going to the teahouse to drink tea in the morning. No matter family or friends gather, they always go to the teahouse to make a pot of tea and have snacks.
