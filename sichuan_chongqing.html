Sichuan-Chongqing cuisine

Sichuan cussion

Sichuan cuisine has a variety of flavors, known as "100 cuisines and 100 flavors", including hemp, hot, sweet, salty, sour and bitter. Sichuan cuisine is characterized by its spicy, fragrant, fresh, and its emphasis on "three peppers" (hot pepper, Chinese prickly ash and Sichuan pepper) and fresh ginger. It is very difficult to modulate these complex flavors, but if you master their recipes and modulation methods, you can easily make delicious food like professional chefs.

Chongqing Hotpot

Chongqing hot pot has an early origin. Different from Beijing Hotpot, it emphasizes the feeling of hot and spicy food.
